Daniel's

In 2018, Daniel formed a mentoring group called Daniel’s Army. By communicating with kids from around the world via Twitter about topics like transitioning out of high school and choosing a career path, Daniel had the idea of creating a forum that these kids could learn from. What started as a 7 person group message grew into a group of 15 young individuals from around the country that Daniel mentors through personal communication and educational trips catered to each members passions.

In addition to receiving direct support from Daniel, each member is able to rely on one another to learn how to be better innovators and grow as young leaders who will become influential adults. Daniel’s Army is designed to allow a way for up and coming creative minds to learn from Daniel’s experiences and network of professional industry colleagues.
